# Lönmeter - Technical Feature & Financial Logic Analysis

Lönmeter is a specialized fleet management and financial automation platform designed for Swedish ride-hailing operations (Bolt, Uber). It bridges the gap between raw platform revenue and compliant payroll processing.

---

## 1. Core Financial Logic (The "Calculation Engine")

The platform implements a precise mathematical model for Swedish taxi operations. All logic is centralized in `lib/services/calculation_service.dart`.

### A. Revenue Deductions (Top-Down)
1.  **VAT (Moms):** Standardized at 6%. 
    *   *Formula:* `Moms = Gross Revenue * 0.056604` (Equivalent to `Gross - (Gross / 1.06)`)
2.  **Platform Fee:** Variable percentage (e.g., 20% for Bolt) applied to the Gross Revenue.
3.  **Company Net Revenue:** The remaining revenue after VAT and Platform Fees.
    *   *Formula:* `Net Revenue = Gross - (Moms + Platform Fee)`

### B. Payroll & Employer Costs
Lönmeter calculates the "Total Payroll Cost" (Total Lönekostnad) which includes:
1.  **Driver Provision:** The base commission (e.g., 35-45% of Net Revenue).
2.  **Holiday Pay (Semesterersättning):** 12% of the Provision.
3.  **Social Fees / Pension:** 4.5% (FORA) or 31.42% (Arbetsgivaravgifter) of the (Provision + Holiday Pay).
    *   *Total Cost:* `Provision + Holiday Pay + Social Fees`

### C. Driver Payout (The "Take-Home" Pay)
1.  **Gross Salary:** `Provision + 100% Tips` (Tips are pass-through and not subject to company fees).
2.  **Preliminary Tax:** 30% flat tax deducted from the Gross Salary.
3.  **Net Payout:** The amount actually transferred to the driver.

## 2. Key Features & Workflows

### Scenario 1: Automated Bolt Fleet Synchronization
*   **Discovery:** The system uses Client ID/Secret to fetch all drivers and trips automatically.
*   **Sync Logic:** `supabase/functions/sync-bolt-fleet` fetches data and applies Scenario 1A/B logic before storing it in the `earnings` table.
*   **Deduplication:** Uses unique transaction IDs to prevent double-counting of trips.

### Scenario 2: Universal Data Import
*   **Template-Based:** Supports CSV imports for Uber, WeCab, and legacy Bolt reports.
*   **Column Mapping:** Allows admins to map arbitrary CSV columns to Lönmeter's internal schema (`brutto`, `tips`, `fee`).
*   **Validation:** Sanity checks for date formats and numeric values.

### Scenario 3: Driver Transparency (Live Worksheet)
*   **Interactive Simulation:** Located in `DriverDetailScreen`, this allows admins to adjust rates (Commission %, Tax %) in real-time to show drivers exactly how their payout is derived.
*   **Breakdown:** Visualizes the "Profit vs. Cost" relationship for every SEK earned.

### Scenario 4: Temporal Dashboard Reporting
*   **Global Filters:** The entire app (Dashboard, Drivers, Reports) respects a global date range filter (Month/Year).
*   **Aggregation:** Real-time summation of hundreds of trips into a single "Monthly Payroll" object.
